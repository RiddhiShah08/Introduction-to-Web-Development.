BEWARE OF PHISHING ATTACKS:

- Do not give your LDAP USERID & PASSWORD to anyone.
- Do not enter LDAP login information in any pop up window.
- Do not reply to any mail asking your login information for any reason.
- Do not enter your LDAP login information on any unknown website.
- Phishing is fraudulent attempt, usually made through email seeking your userid, password and personal information.
- If you receive any such email, Please report here immediately.
- Immediately change your passwords if you have accidently revealed your credentials.
- Computer Center never ask you for such information.
